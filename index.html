Our Specials

Flavor Phở Combination

A steaming, fragrant Vietnamese beef broth soup with noodles, bean sprouts, scallions and basil.

Egg Rolls

A cylindrical, savory roll with shredded cabbage, chopped pork, and other fillings inside a thickly-wrapped wheat flour skin, which is fried in hot oil.

Shaking Beef

a dish of French-inspired Vietnamese cuisine that consists of beef sauteed with cucumber, lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, pepper, and soy sauce. The beef is cut into small cubes the size of playing dice before being sauteed.

Our Story

Established in 2016. We opened on the 3rd February 2016, taking over from a previous Pho77 restaurant. The restaurant has a new owners and a new menu.

Anthony Trang and his cousin/partner Tracy having spent some years both owning and operating other f&b businesses from Vietnamese to Pretzels have always wanted to have a pho restaurant here in LA. After searching for a great location they came across this warm and welcoming dining destination, Fountain Plaza here in Cerritos. After renovating the restaurant from the interior to the menu, they are now serving their own authentic Vietnamese food.
